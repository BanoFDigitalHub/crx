Frequently Asked Questions
How do I start earning from referrals?
Share your referral code with your friends or community. When someone registers using your code and activates a plan, they’ll count as your referral. You start earning once you complete your first 5 active referrals.
When will I get my $1 reward?
Once your 5 referred users have successfully registered and activated a plan, you’ll instantly receive $1 in your wallet. You can withdraw it anytime or let it grow with more referrals.
What happens when I reach 15 or 100 referrals?
After 15 referrals, you’ll officially become a Team Leader and start receiving a separate salary from the company. When you reach 100 referrals, you’ll also receive a special company gift in addition to your Team Leader position and salary.
Is there any limit to how much I can earn?
No limit at all! You can invite unlimited users. Every 5 new active referrals add to your rewards and unlock bigger milestones and positions.
